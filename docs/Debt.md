# Debt

The Debt page tracks outstanding debts (credit cards, loans, mortgages, etc.) and projects their repayment over time.

## Overview

The Debt tab includes a **Filter Bar** at the top to select the month. The summary cards and table reflect balances as of the selected month, with historical balances estimated via amortisation for past months.

Each debt entry shows:

- The current outstanding balance (with a change indicator vs the previous month)
- The APR and monthly interest charge
- Minimum payment, overpayment, and your share of the monthly payment
- Click a debt row to expand the full repayment projection and any deal periods

The **Total Debt** and **Monthly Payments** summary cards also show change indicators vs the previous month.

For step-by-step instructions on adding a debt, see [[Adding Debts\|Adding-Debts]].

For information on repayment strategies, deal periods, and overpayments, see [[Debt Payoff Strategies\|Debt-Payoff-Strategies]].

For the debt projection and debt-to-income charts, see [[Debt Charts\|Debt-Charts]].

## Editing and Deleting

Click the **edit icon** on any debt card to update it. Click the **delete icon** to remove it permanently.

## Household Debts

Debts flagged as **Household Debt** appear on the [Household](Household) summary page. The combined balance is shown alongside other household financial data.
