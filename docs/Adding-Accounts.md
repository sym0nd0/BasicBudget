# Adding Accounts

Payment accounts represent the bank accounts, credit cards, or wallets you use to pay for expenses. Assigning expenses to accounts lets you filter the Dashboard and track which account covers which spending.

## Managing Payment Accounts

Go to **Settings** in the sidebar, then scroll to the **Payment Accounts** section.

### Adding an Account

1. Click **Add Account**.
2. Enter a name (e.g. "Barclays Current", "Amex Credit Card").
3. Tick **Joint account** if all household members should be able to use the account on expenses.
4. Click **Add Account**.

### Editing an Account

Click the **edit icon** next to any account to update its name or joint-account flag.

### Deleting an Account

Click the **delete icon** next to an account to remove it.

> **Note:** Deleting an account does not delete the expenses linked to it. Those expenses will become unassigned (no account selected).

### Joint Accounts

Tick **Joint account** to make the account available to all household members when they create or edit expenses.

## Assigning Expenses to Accounts

When adding or editing an expense, select the account from the **Account** dropdown. The Expenses table shows the linked account for each row.
