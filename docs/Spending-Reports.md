# Spending Reports

The Spending section of the [[Reports]] page shows how your money is being spent across categories over the selected time range.

## Expense Breakdown Donut

A donut chart showing total spending by category over the selected period.

- Each segment represents a category proportional to its share of total spending
- A legend table alongside the chart shows each category's total amount and percentage
- Hover over a segment to see the exact amount

This chart helps identify which categories consume the largest share of your budget.

## Category Trends Stacked Area Chart

A stacked area chart showing how spending in each category has changed month by month.

- The x-axis shows months within the selected time range
- The y-axis shows total spending in pounds
- Each category is shown as a coloured band stacked on top of the others
- Hover over any point to see the breakdown for that month

This chart is useful for spotting categories that are growing over time, or identifying months where specific categories spiked.

## Colour Palettes

If you have a colour vision impairment, you can switch to an accessible palette in [[Settings]] → Colour Blindness Palettes. The donut and stacked area charts will use the selected palette.
