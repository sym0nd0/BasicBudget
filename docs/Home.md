# BasicBudget Documentation

Welcome to the BasicBudget wiki. BasicBudget is a self-hosted, multi-user personal finance application for tracking monthly income and expenses, managing debts with full repayment schedules, monitoring savings goals, and visualising your household's financial picture through interactive charts.
