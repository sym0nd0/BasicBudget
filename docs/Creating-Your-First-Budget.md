# Creating Your First Budget

This walkthrough guides you through setting up your first monthly budget in BasicBudget.

## Step 1: Select a Month

The filter bar at the top of every page contains a **Month** selector. It defaults to the current month. All data you add will be associated with the active month unless you set start/end dates.

## Step 2: Add Your Income

1. Go to **Income** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Add Income**.
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Name** — e.g. "Salary", "Freelance Work"
   - **Amount** — your take-home amount in pounds
   - **Gross / Net** — select **Net** if you entered take-home pay
   - **Recurrence** — select **Monthly** for a regular salary
   - **Posting Day** — the day of the month you receive it (e.g. 25)
4. Click **Save**.

Repeat for each income source. For more detail, see [[Income]].

## Step 3: Add Your Expenses

1. Go to **Expenses** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Add Expense**.
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Name** — e.g. "Rent", "Groceries"
   - **Amount** — the amount in pounds
   - **Category** — select the appropriate category
   - **Type** — **Fixed** for predictable costs, **Variable** for amounts that change
   - **Recurrence** — **Monthly** for regular bills
   - **Posting Day** — the day the expense is due
4. Click **Save**.

Repeat for each expense. For more detail, see [[Expenses]].

## Step 4: View the Dashboard

Go to **Dashboard** in the sidebar. You will see:

- **Total Income** — sum of all active income for the month
- **Total Expenses** — sum of all active expenses for the month
- **Disposable Income** — income minus expenses
- **Income vs Outgoings** bar chart
- **Expense breakdown** donut chart

## Step 5: Add Debts and Savings (Optional)

- Go to **Debt** to record credit cards, loans, or other outstanding balances.
- Go to **Savings** to set up savings goals with target amounts and monthly contributions.

## Tips

- Use **Start Date** and **End Date** on income and expense entries to limit them to specific months (useful for one-off or temporary items).
- Mark items as **Household** to share them with household members on the Household summary page.
- Use **Month Locking** in Settings to freeze past months once you have finished reviewing them.
