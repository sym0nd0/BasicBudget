# Net Worth Tracking

While BasicBudget does not calculate a single net worth figure, it provides the building blocks to track your overall financial position over time.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Where to find it |
|---|---|
| **Total savings balance** | Dashboard summary card — Total Savings |
| **Total debt balance** | Dashboard summary card — Total Debt |
| **Monthly disposable income** | Reports → Budget Performance → Disposable Income chart |
| **Savings rate trend** | Reports → Budget Performance → Savings Rate chart |
| **Debt reduction over time** | Reports → Debt Charts → Debt Projection chart |

## Tracking Progress

A simple way to monitor your financial position:

1. **Monitor debt reduction** — the Debt Projection chart shows your total debt balance falling over time as you make payments.
2. **Track savings growth** — update the Current Amount on each savings goal as you contribute to see progress bars advance.
3. **Watch your savings rate** — the Reports savings rate chart shows the percentage of income you are retaining each month.
4. **Review disposable income** — the disposable income trend shows whether you are consistently living within your means.

## Month Locking for Historical Accuracy

Once you have reviewed a past month, lock it in [[Settings]] → Month Locking. This prevents historical data from being accidentally edited, giving you an accurate record over time.

## Exporting Data

For deeper analysis in a spreadsheet, export your data as JSON. See [[Exporting Data\|Exporting-Data]].
